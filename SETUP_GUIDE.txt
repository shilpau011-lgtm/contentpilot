# ContentPilot — Setup Guide
# Your 4-Channel AI Video Studio
# Zero cost after setup (except $5/month ElevenLabs if you prefer it)

==========================================================
  WHAT YOU HAVE AFTER SETUP
==========================================================

4 YouTube channels running automatically:
  🧠 Dark Psychology
  🌌 Space & Universe
  📜 Dark History
  🕵️ True Crime

Each generates 5 videos/week = 20 videos/week total
Your only job: review & approve from your phone

==========================================================
  STEP 1 — GET YOUR FREE API KEY
==========================================================

1. Go to console.anthropic.com
2. Sign up / log in
3. Click "API Keys" → Create new key
4. Copy the key (starts with sk-ant-api03-)
5. You get free credits to start — enough for weeks of testing

==========================================================
  STEP 2 — GITHUB SETUP (your free cloud server)
==========================================================

1. Go to github.com → Create free account
2. Click "New Repository"
3. Name it: contentpilot
4. Set to PRIVATE (important — keeps your content secret)
5. Upload all files from this folder to the repo

To add your API key securely:
1. In your repo → Settings → Secrets and variables → Actions
2. Click "New repository secret"
3. Name: ANTHROPIC_API_KEY
4. Value: your key from Step 1
5. Save

For Pexels (free stock footage):
1. Go to pexels.com/api → Create free account
2. Copy your API key
3. Add to GitHub secrets as: PEXELS_API_KEY

==========================================================
  STEP 3 — OPEN YOUR DASHBOARD
==========================================================

Option A — Open locally:
1. Open dashboard/index.html in your browser
2. Enter your Anthropic API key when prompted
3. Click "Generate Today's Scripts"

Option B — Host on Netlify (free, access from phone):
1. Go to netlify.com → Sign up free
2. Drag the "dashboard" folder to Netlify Drop
3. Get a URL like: https://contentpilot-xyz.netlify.app
4. Open on your phone anytime

==========================================================
  STEP 4 — ADD YOUR BOYFRIEND'S VOICE
==========================================================

When ready:
1. Find a quiet room — no background noise
2. Record 60 seconds of him reading naturally
   (use iPhone Voice Memos or any recorder)
3. Export as: voice_reference.wav
4. Upload to the config/ folder in your GitHub repo
5. Agent 2 will automatically clone it next run

Recording script suggestion (read naturally, conversationally):
---
"Welcome. Today we're going to explore something most people 
never stop to think about. The patterns that shape our decisions, 
our relationships, and our understanding of the world around us.
What you're about to discover may change the way you see 
everything. Let's begin. Some of the most fascinating discoveries 
in science started with a simple question. Why do people behave 
the way they do? The answer, it turns out, is far more complex
and far more interesting than anyone expected."
---

==========================================================
  STEP 5 — YOUR DAILY ROUTINE
==========================================================

Every weekday morning:
1. GitHub Actions runs automatically at 6am Dubai time
2. Agent 1 writes 4 scripts (one per channel)
3. You get content in your dashboard

Your job (5-10 mins):
1. Open dashboard on your phone
2. Read each script
3. Tap APPROVE ✅ or REJECT ❌
4. Done — everything else is automatic

After approval:
→ Agent 2 generates the voice narration
→ Agent 3 assembles the video
→ Video scheduled to post at optimal time
→ You can check the schedule anytime
